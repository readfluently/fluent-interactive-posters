Photosynthesis in 4 Steps

1. Sunlight

The plant gets energy from the Sun.

2. Water

The roots absorb water from the soil.

3. Carbon dioxide

The leaves take in carbon dioxide from the air.

4. Food is made

The plant makes glucose and releases oxygen.
